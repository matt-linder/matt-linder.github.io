Our group decided that one of the most common problems on campus is the long wait times at retail locations such as Chic-fil-a, Panda Express, and Strom Thurmond Fitness Center.

These are some of the many ideas we decided we could implement into our app. These features range from point systems to in depth analytics regarding wait times.
